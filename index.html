Our Comprehensive Services

We provide complete diagnostic and therapeutic solutions for hearing and speech disorders

Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA)

Comprehensive hearing assessment to measure your hearing sensitivity across different frequencies.

Impedance Audiometry

Evaluates middle ear function and helps diagnose conditions like fluid buildup or eardrum perforation.

OAE (Otoacoustic Emissions)

Screening test that checks the function of the inner ear (cochlea), especially useful for newborns.

BERA / ABR

Objective test that measures how the hearing nerves and brain respond to sound.

BC ABR

Bone conduction testing to determine the type of hearing loss (conductive vs. sensorineural).

Hearing Aid Fitting & Trial

Professional fitting and adjustment of digital hearing aids with trial periods available.

Hearing Aid Servicing

Maintenance, cleaning, and repair services to keep your hearing aids functioning optimally.

Speech Therapy for Children & Adults

Individualized therapy for articulation, fluency, and other speech disorders across all ages.

Language Delay Therapy

Specialized intervention for children experiencing delays in language development.

Voice Therapy

Treatment for voice disorders including hoarseness, vocal nodules, and vocal cord dysfunction.
