Our Services

Comprehensive care for all your speech and hearing needs

Speech Therapy

Professional speech therapy for articulation, fluency, voice disorders, and language development.

  • Articulation disorders
  • Stuttering and fluency
  • Voice therapy
  • Language delays

Hearing Assessments

Comprehensive hearing evaluations and diagnostic testing for all ages.

  • Hearing tests
  • Tympanometry
  • Hearing aid fittings
  • Hearing protection

Pediatric Care

Specialized services for children with speech, language, and hearing challenges.

  • Early intervention
  • School-age therapy
  • Developmental assessments
  • Parent training

Cognitive Communication

Treatment for communication difficulties related to neurological conditions.

  • Stroke recovery
  • Traumatic brain injury
  • Memory and attention
  • Swallowing therapy
